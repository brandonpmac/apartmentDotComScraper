About 403 West

Up to One Month Free on our Conventional Four Bedroom Renovated Homes - Now Available!Whether you live an active, fast-paced lifestyle or prefer to slow things down, the newly renovated apartment homes at 403 West offer an amenities package and in-home finishes you deserve. Our Raleigh, NC community includes amenities fit for either lifestyle. Our 24-hour fitness center with Yoga studio, inviting Work from Home spaces and Resident Clubhouse with gaming areas provide dynamic spaces fit just for you!Our quaint homes offer space to relax and renew after a busy day. Step into your newly renovated apartment home and you are met with plank-inspired wood flooring, a stainless-steel amenities package and neutral designs for easy decorating. Stay easily organized with our over-sized laundry rooms and walk-in closets. Youll feel rejuvenated from the abundance of natural light and views of charming courtyards and lush landscaping. Each home offers large picture windows, oversized patios* and balconies* and versatile spaces crafted for you.

403 West is an apartment community located in Wake County and the 27606 ZIP Code. This area is served by the Wake County attendance zone.

Neighborhood

Southwest Raleigh borders the scenic campus of North Carolina State University and houses many shopping centers and apartment complexes to accommodate students. The western edge of the neighborhood gives way to more established subdivisions full of families and professionals. Residents enjoy easy access to major expressways, Downtown Raleigh, and nearby Cary. Sporting events and festivals put on by the university are a major benefit to living in this suburb. Considering its proximity to major amenities, Southwest Raleigh stays relatively quiet and encompasses many natural areas, lakes, and green spaces for locals to enjoy the outdoors and Raleighs temperate climate.
